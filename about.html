About Us

Friendly, reliable computer repairs you can trust.

Emre Computer Repairs is a UK-based IT support service helping home users and small businesses keep their computers running smoothly. We offer fast turnaround, honest pricing and clear communication from start to finish.

We believe computer repairs should be simple and stress-free. No jargon, no surprise bills — just fast, professional service from a technician who genuinely cares about getting your device working again.

Why Choose Us?

  • Transparent pricing — you know the cost before we start
  • No fix, no charge on diagnostic assessments
  • Fast turnaround — most repairs completed same or next day
  • Home visits available within 25 and 35 miles
  • Remote support available — no need to leave your home
  • Friendly, jargon-free advice every step of the way
  • Experienced with Windows PCs, laptops and Mac OS

Coverage
